👨‍🔧 Your Technician

Meet Aydan Ohlson

SafeHands Auto Care is built on one simple idea: your time is valuable. Instead of spending half your day at a shop, let us bring professional auto service directly to your driveway.

Aydan completed the Automotive Technology Certificate at Raritan Valley Community College — a rigorous one-year program covering fuel systems, electrical systems, engine analysis, suspension, and wheel alignment. The program prepares graduates for the ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) certification exam.

Trained in diagnostics, brake systems, transmission service, suspension & steering, engine performance, and climate control — Aydan brings classroom expertise and hands-on skill to every job.

Operating under SafeHands Home & Tech Repair LLC — licensed & insured.
